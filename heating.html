Heating Services

H2O plumbing and gas have been specializing in boiler break downs and fault diagnostics and pride our self on being prompt and efficient at diagnosing boiler problems. As well as providing advice that is best for our customers and not just our company. Whether it be on a new boiler, a full heating upgrade or something you’re not sure about.

We aim to carry out all work with the minimal amount of disruption to our customers as well as ensuring the engineers tidy up after the work is complete leaving the house as they found it. All work will be finished to the highest standards with at least 12 months guarantee, and all new boilers registered with the building inspector, gas safe and the manufacture.

We are able to offer you the home owner a yearly gas safety check to your boiler and gas appliances. Helping them to maintain their optimum efficiency, as well as giving you the knowledge that your boiler is running as it should be.

Over the age of 60 then we could save you up to £300 on a new boiler, so why not give us a call to find out what you may be entitled to.

We can help with:

  • Boiler Breakdowns
  • Diagnosis Of Boiler Problems
  • New Boilers
  • Boiler Repairs
