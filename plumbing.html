Plumbing Services

We offer a specialized service in all domestic and commercial plumbing, whether it a small repair to a full new installation.

H2O can carry out all types of plumbing work from a small leak on a tap you have not had time to fix, through to a comprehensive domestic or commercial installation.

We aim to carry out all work with minimal disruption and a time that is convenient to both ourselves and you our customer. All our plumbers ensure they tidy up after completion of the work. Leaving the house has they found it.



Most commonly we work on:

  • Leaks, burst pipes, repairs to taps and drain blockages
  • Appliance installations
  • Stockcocks & air locks
  • Tank issues
  • toiler repairs & installations
  • Sanflo repairs & installs
  • Immersion Heaters
  • Shower & bath repairs
  • Radiator power flushing
